Cajun Corn Maque Choux

This traditional Lousianan dish is a delicious blend of vegtables, influenced by Creole and American Indian culture.

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cups of frozen shoepeg corn, thawed
  • 2 plum tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 pound of andouille sausage, cooked and diced
  • 1/4 cup of chopped green onion tops
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions

  1. Saut√© onion and bell pepper in hot oil in a large skillet over medium heat for 8 minutes or until tender.
  2. Add corn, tomato, and sausage; cook stirring often for 15 minutes.
  3. Stir in green onions, salt, and peper; cook for 5 minutes

Prep time: 10 minutes, Cook time: 30 minutes

Makes: 6 servings
